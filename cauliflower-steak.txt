Ingredients:
 - 1 head cauliflower
 - Salt
 - Ground pepper
 - 2 leaves sage, chopped
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
 1. Slice the cauliflower through the center
 2. Season the cauliflower with pepper and sage
 3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat
 4. Add olive oil and place cauliflower in the pan
 5. switch the cauliflower for 3-4 minutes per side until golden
